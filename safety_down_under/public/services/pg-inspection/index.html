services

We inspect playgrounds.

We provide playground safety audits and maintenance inspections.

We offer a detailed inspection of the playground equipment at your facility. Our report will identify any know hazards and offer a recommended course of action. CPSC 325 guidelines and ASTM F 1487 standards will be followed. The report will also include detailed photographs. Inspections are completed by two Certified Playground Safety Inspectors.
